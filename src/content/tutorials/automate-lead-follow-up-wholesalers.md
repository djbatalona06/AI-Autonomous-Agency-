Automating seller-lead follow-up means firing an instant, personalized multi-channel message (SMS + email) the moment a lead comes in, then running an automated 14-day cadence that flips the lead to "Hot" the second they reply. This closes the single biggest gap in wholesaling: the minutes between a seller filling out a form and a human actually calling them back.

## Why speed-to-lead is the whole game

Motivated sellers rarely wait around. If your team's average response time is measured in hours, a faster competitor is already on the phone with that seller. Manual follow-up also degrades under volume — the leads that come in on a busy day get the same shallow effort as the ones that come in on a slow day, when they deserve more.

## What the automation actually does

1. **Instant multi-channel touch.** The moment a lead lands (from your website, a landing page, or an ad), the system sends a personalized SMS and email referencing the property address and offer, no template-blast feel.
2. **14-day cadence.** If the seller doesn't reply, a scheduled sequence of follow-ups continues — spaced to stay persistent without becoming spam.
3. **Auto-escalation.** The moment the seller replies, the automation flags the lead Hot in your CRM and notifies the acquisitions rep, so a human takes over the conversation exactly when it matters.
