Automating lead capture means wiring your forms, ads, and inbound channels directly into your CRM so every lead is enriched, deduplicated, and assigned to a rep within seconds — with no one manually copying a spreadsheet row into a contact record.

## Why manual lead entry costs you deals

Every minute between a lead filling out a form and a rep reaching out is a minute a competitor could get there first. Manual entry also introduces duplicate contacts, missing fields, and leads that sit in an inbox over a weekend.

## What the automation does

1. **Capture.** Every inbound channel (web form, landing page, ad platform) feeds into a single intake pipeline.
2. **Enrich + dedupe.** The lead is matched against existing CRM records and enriched with available firmographic or contact data before it's created.
3. **Route + alert.** The lead lands in the CRM assigned to the right rep, who gets pinged immediately — no lead sits unclaimed.
