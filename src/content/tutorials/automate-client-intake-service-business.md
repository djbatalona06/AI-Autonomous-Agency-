Automating client intake means connecting your inquiry form directly to your booking calendar and CRM, so every new inquiry becomes either a booked appointment or a one-click-bookable time slot — with the contact logged automatically and zero manual data entry.

## The problem with manual intake

In a 5–50 person service business, intake is usually a person checking a shared inbox, copying details into a spreadsheet or CRM, then emailing back and forth to find a time that works. Every one of those steps is a place a lead can go cold, and the process doesn't scale on a busy day.

## What the automation does

1. **Capture.** The inquiry form (website, Facebook lead ad, or phone intake) feeds directly into the automation — no manual copy-paste.
2. **Enrich + log.** The contact is created or matched in your CRM automatically, deduplicated against existing clients.
3. **Book.** Depending on your setup, the system either books the appointment directly against calendar availability or sends a one-click scheduling link, with a confirmation sent immediately.
